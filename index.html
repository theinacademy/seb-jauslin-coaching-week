About the event

What this is about

International Coaching Week is the one moment each year the global coaching community pauses to reflect on what the profession is actually for. This year, two leadership coaches — one rooted in European corporate culture, one in Silicon Valley and neuroscience — will sit down together for an open, unscripted conversation. No slides. No pitch. Just honest thinking on what genuinely transforms leaders in 2026, and what gets in the way.

What you'll take away

  • What coaching-led transformation actually looks like in practice — seen from two continents and two professional cultures.
  • Practical frameworks for building trust, making decisions under pressure, and developing teams that perform — without burning out.
  • A live, bilingual session you can engage in directly — ask questions, share your own experience, and leave with something concrete.
